Makerz is a Malaysian footwear brand established in 2019 by founder Boss Wan. The company operates under the registered name Makerz Empire (003261761-W). Based in Petaling Jaya, Selangor, Makerz has gained popularity among the younger generation for its unique and appealing sneaker designs. The brand offers a diverse range of sneakers, including models like OL Asas Pasang, OL Asas Surut, Purnama, and Rentaka. Each design is crafted with quality materials, emphasizing user comfort with soft and comfortable soles. In addition to footwear, Makerz has expanded its product line to include clothing and accessories, such as shirts and caps.
